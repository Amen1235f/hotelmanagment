Pico Hotel – Hotel Manager & PHP Script

PicoHotel is an Ultimate Hotel Booking Solution that comes with PHP Laravel. It’s developed for those people who want to start their hotel business website. Users can book rooms from the website and that will be considered a booking request. Admin or receptionist needs to approve that request by assigning rooms. Users can pay via online payment systems.
